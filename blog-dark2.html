About HP LIFE: AI for Beginners

Offered by HP LIFE (Learning Initiative for Entrepreneurs), AI for Beginners is a self-paced course designed to introduce learners to the fundamentals of Artificial Intelligence, practical use cases, and its potential in everyday business decisions.

  • 🧠 Absolute Beginners Learn what AI really is (and what it’s not), how it’s already affecting industries, and how small businesses or individuals can start exploring it confidently.
  • 📈 Entrepreneurs & Innovators Discover how AI can streamline tasks, enhance decision-making, and power smarter operations without needing a technical background.
  • 🌐 Global Learners Available for free, anytime, in multiple languages. Includes real-life examples, reflection exercises, and a digital certificate of completion.
